Official Discord Server Rules

Violating these guidelines will typically lead to a permanent ban. We permit discussion and use of client modifications such as BetterDiscord and Vencord.

1. Adherence to Discord's Guidelines

You must strictly adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

  • **Age and Technology (1.1, 1.2):** Members must meet Discord's minimum age (13+). Do not use self-bots, API exploits, or any actions harmful to Discord's servers.
  • **Security Threats (1.3):** Do not engage in, organize, or joke about raids, doxxing, or swatting. Sharing doxxing information is also strictly forbidden. Report issues via ticket.
  • **Malicious Content (1.4):** Do not send malware, viruses, malicious links, scams, or IP grabbers. Discussion of these topics is restricted to specific channels and must not involve sharing source code or teaching creation methods.
  • **Privacy (1.5, 1.6):** Do not leak any private, identifiable information (e.g., IPs, addresses, legal names). No recording of voice channels (VCs) without the consent of everyone present.

2. No NSFW Content / Gore / Explicit Behavior

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy in this area due to the presence of minors.

  • **Sexual & Shocking Content (2.1, 2.2, 2.4, 2.5):** Absolutely no content, conversation, jokes, or media that is NSFW, NSFL (gore), or suggestive. This includes cropped images, optical illusions, and any media implying explicit content.
  • **Flirting and E-Dating (2.3):** Do not engage in e-dating or flirting. This includes sending media or making comments with the intent of "hitting on" or "rizzing up" other members. Do not enter the server with the intent to find people to flirt with.
  • **Sexualizing Minors (2.6):** Zero tolerance for any joke or comment that sexualizes a minor. If you flirt with someone who is found to be a minor, you will be punished.

3. Conduct and Respect

Maintain a civil and respectful environment for all members.

  • **Positive Atmosphere (3.1):** Do not troll, be toxic, be rude, or cause arguments. If you are unable to be kind and respectful, you will face punishment. Take arguments to DMs or avoid them entirely.
  • **Hate Speech (3.2):** Zero tolerance for all forms of hate speech (racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia). This includes profile symbols (like the swastika) and death threats (even if meant as a joke).
  • **Sensitive Topics (3.3, 3.4):** Avoid topics like politics, religion, war, sexuality, or gender if they lead to drama. Do not ragebait by intentionally provoking controversial discussions.

4. Server Organization

Keep the server running smoothly by avoiding disruptive actions.

  • **Spamming (4.1):** Do not spam chat, media, reactions, pings, or flood channels. VC spamming includes excessive soundboards, screaming, or rapidly joining/leaving.
  • **Nuisance Behavior (4.2, 4.3):** Avoid irritating or annoying numerous members. Do not send loud ("ear-rape") or excessively bright media that could trigger photosensitivity issues.
  • **Moderation Bypass (4.4, 4.5):** Do not mini-mod (correct others' behavior, threaten punishment, or argue with mod actions). Use the report system. Do not attempt to bypass Automod filters using altered spelling.

5. Profiles and Advertising

  • **Profiles (6.1, 6.2):** Your entire Discord profile must adhere to all server rules. Do not impersonate staff or key figures.
  • **Advertising (7.1, 7.2):** DM advertising is prohibited. All promotional content (links, invites) must be posted only in the designated advertising channels.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of community is Planet Xplo?

Planet Xplo is a large, general-purpose digital home centered around **gaming, content creation, and real-time events**. We host regular voice chat streams, movie nights, game tournaments, and provide spaces for artists and creators to showcase their work.

What is the minimum age requirement to join the server?

As per Discord's Terms of Service, you must be at least **13 years old** to join Planet Xplo. Due to our zero-tolerance policy against NSFW content and gore, we strictly enforce compliance with all Discord guidelines.

How can I apply to become a staff or moderator?

Staff applications are opened periodically. The best way to be noticed is to be an active, positive, and helpful member of the community. When applications are open, links will be posted in the #announcements channel.

What are "Rank Roles" and how do I get them?

Rank Roles are custom, exclusive titles and colors you earn automatically based on your activity level (messages sent, time in VC) within the server. They are a fun way to show off your seniority and dedication! They are obtained passively just by participating.

I was banned. Can I appeal the decision?

Yes, we believe in fairness. If you were muted, warned, or banned and believe it was an error, you can submit a formal appeal using the link in the Moderation & Transparency section.

Do you host live events, game nights, or streams?

Absolutely! We host multiple live events every week, including movie nights, game tournaments (with prizes!), and Q&A sessions with the server owner. Check the #events channel and keep an eye on our Voice Channels for live streams and broadcasts!

Is Planet Xplo completely free to join and use?

Yes, joining and participating in Planet Xplo is entirely free! We do not require any payment or subscription. We are supported by optional, non-intrusive methods like YouTube ad revenue and occasional sponsored events. Any paid features (like Discord Server Boosts) are purely voluntary.

What is the expected etiquette in Voice Channels (VCs)?

In our general VCs, we ask that you maintain a civil tone, avoid excessive screaming or ear-rape noises, and use push-to-talk if you have a loud background. Please be respectful of the conversation flow. Specific VCs (like music or stream rooms) may have additional pinned rules.

I just joined. Which channel should I start with?

Welcome! We recommend heading straight to the #rules channel for a quick review, then jump into #general chat to introduce yourself and say hi. You can also check out #roles to grab some self-assignable roles!

How can I talk directly with the server owner, XploPlayZYTube?

The owner, XploPlayZYTube, frequently hosts live Voice Chat Q&A sessions. These are the best times to interact directly and ask him questions about his content, the server, or anything else! Keep an eye on the #announcements and #events channels for scheduled times.
